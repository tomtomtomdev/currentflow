RULE A — Tradeability gate (LD-2)
The Wyckoff phase classifier is a hard gate before scoring at step [3]. Only Accumulation Phase C or D is tradeable. Detectors feed it; nothing bypasses it.
RULE B — Presentation gate (LD-9)
A module may show SMS / probability / rank / buy-sell claim only after it survives paper validation. Until earned: render the observation, no number attached. The paper engine is the sole promoter.
